About Me

With over 2 years of experience teaching English to students of all levels, I specialize in personalizing the learning experience, especially for beginners, and helping students achieve their language goals. I hold a BA in Humanities from Bard College (German-American double degree), where I've studied in English and partially in German.

My teaching philosophy centers on creating an engaging and supportive learning environment where students can build confidence in their English language skills. I am very open to working with students from a wide variety of cultural backgrounds.
